# Key Findings

## Churn Patterns

- Customers with monthly contracts demonstrated higher churn rates.
- Customers with higher monthly charges were more likely to leave.
- Longer-tenure customers showed stronger retention.

## Business Recommendations

1. Encourage annual contract adoption.
2. Improve retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
3. Provide loyalty incentives for long-term customers.
4. Monitor high monthly charge accounts proactively.
